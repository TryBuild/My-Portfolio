Integrated Health Clinic

This Project showcases

A modern design for a Health Clinic system that the patient can book and reschedule their appointments with health care providers. Healthcare providers and patients can chat with each other.

Log in and Register function

Both patients and healthcare providers have their account to log in or register to have an account if they don't have one. It helps the patients and healthcare providers to chat with each other and also for other purposes that can be useful to have an account.

Explanation:

1. Start: A patient sends a request to book an appointment, the process begins.
2. Check Provider Availability: The availability of the provider is checked.
3. Booking Process: If the provider is available, an appointment entry is added.
4. Unavailability: If the provider is not available, the patient gets notified.
5. Reschedule or Cancel: The patient can reschedule or cancel the appointment.
6. End: The process ends with a confirmed, rescheduled, or canceled appointment.
